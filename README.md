## What It Enables

- **North America drives 52% of revenue** — but that concentration is a risk, not just a win. Over-reliance on a single region leaves the business exposed if that market softens.
- **Direct channel generates 15.7K of 20K orders** — the acquisition strategy is almost entirely organic. Social Media accounts for just 0.3K orders, representing significant untapped growth potential across paid and affiliate channels.
- **Revenue peaks every Q4 without exception** — this pattern is consistent enough to anchor confident seasonal inventory and promotional planning.
- **The 27in 4K Gaming Monitor alone drives $1.77M** — nearly 32% of total revenue from a single product, a concentration risk worth addressing through accessory bundling or category expansion.

---

## Recommendations

- **Test paid marketing in EMEA before the Q4 peak.** EMEA sits at $1.7M with growth headroom — a targeted email or affiliate push in Q3 could compound the seasonal spike without heavy investment.
- **Diversify the channel mix.** With Direct dominating and Social Media nearly invisible, even modest investment in paid social or influencer partnerships could meaningfully shift the acquisition mix.
- **Plan inventory around Q4 aggressively.** The seasonality pattern is consistent across all three years — this is a planning advantage, not just an observation.
- **Reduce single-product revenue risk.** Bundle the top gaming monitor with accessories (mouse, headset, keyboard) to spread revenue across the catalogue and increase AOV.
